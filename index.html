University of Houston Cafeteria

We are thrilled to extend a warm welcome to you on the virtual doorstep of the University of Houston Cafeteria! As the hub of campus dining, our website is designed to be your go-to resource for all things culinary at UH.

Fresh Meals Everyday

We are committed to sourcing the freshest ingredients from local farmers and producers. Enjoy the goodness of locally grown fruits, vegetables, and proteins in every bite.

Vegan Options

Whether you are a meat lover, vegetarian, or have dietary restrictions, our new menu caters to everyone. From vibrant salads to protein-packed bowls, there's something for every taste and preference.

Chef Specials

Indulge in our chef's daily specials, carefully crafted to showcase culinary creativity and bring unique flavors to your plate. You won't want to miss out on these limited-time offerings!

Customization Stations

Personalize your meals at our customization stations, where you can choose your favorite ingredients and create a meal that suits your individual taste.
